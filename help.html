Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We are working hard to design a cash out feature which will allow you to cash out manually which we hope to have up and running in the new year in the mean time we will gather your deposit banking details you would like your Royalties you have earned from sales sent over to if you have any questions or would like to update your details you can email Royalties@picsy.co.uk and we will be back in contact in 24 hours or less to update your deposit details or help you with any other concerns.

Here at Picsy we are a young and lively company looking to bring new art to the public and offer amazing canvas's based off your art. We allow you to set the ideal RRP / sale price the end client will pay and give you 10% Royalties payment for each canvas sold on the total value amount / sale price, We have set this Percentage based off feed back from other Artist of what would be a fair amount and in turn allow you to grow your art be it photography or Graphics art work and show the world your love and passion which we then market, print ship to the end customer.

Here at Picsy we bring new Artist onboard each day and get asked this question all the time how does the copy rights work for your images / content you upload. This is where we proud our self on giving you full control of the content you upload we don't own your images you do! and in your dashboard you have full control to remove your uploaded listings any time once this is done we can no longer sell your image on our websites or market it. Once you remove your image it can take up to 24 hours to take effect on our website any sales done well the the listings is still active will still be fulfilled but once your listings is taken off no sale can be done and the image belong to you. Once the canvas is sold of your image this is at the only point your copy right ends meaning the art work / canvas now belongs to the person who brought it but they are not allowed to make copys or re-print it they only have the copy right for that one canvas print. To High light you control use of your image and here at Picsy we never own your image we only market, print and sell the image to be used as a canvas and once your listings has been taken off our website through your control panel it can not be sold. If you have any questions about how we use your images you can email us at Copyright@picsy.co.uk

Here at Picsy we have a very strict rule we do not allow other users to copy other artist works / content this is made very clear in our user agreement which all artist sign before they can use the dashboard and website if you do see an image which you believe belongs to you which you have taken or made we would like you to email us at Copyright@picsy.co.uk and we will look into this straight away. To prove you own the image its always helpful if you can give more information such as where you took the image a link in the email of the listing in question as well as do you have the RAW file for it and any other evidence the image does in fact belong to you if we find the Artist in question has used your image and its the same image as yours their account will be closed straight away as we want to build a family of artist which care about sharing there work and helping them fund more of there amazing images.
